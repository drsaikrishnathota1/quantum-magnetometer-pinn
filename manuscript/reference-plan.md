# Reference Plan for Results in Physics Microarticle

Target: 8–10 references maximum.
Date range: preferably 2023–2026.
Rule: Do not force same-journal references unless directly relevant.

2. J. Amoros-Binefa and J. Kolodynski, "Noisy atomic magnetometry with Kalman filtering and measurement-based feedback," 2024.

## Do not cite
- Random AI papers
- Defense/UAV/GPS papers
- H-1B/immigration/social topics
- Same-journal papers unrelated to sensing, optics, instrumentation, phase extraction, or computational physics
